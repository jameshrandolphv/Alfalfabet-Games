About
Alfalfabet Games is a small, independent game studio that produces simple, challenging arcade games for each letter of the alphabet. We currently support iOS and Android devices, but hope to support Windows phones in the future.
Contact/Support
Please email alfalfabetgames@gmail.com with any questions, advice, criticism, jokes, or inquiries.
Privacy Policy
We value our users' privacy and will never collect any sensitive information without prior consent. In-game high scores are the only information we collect, and these scores are (optionally) submitted to public leaderboards provided by Google Play Game Services and Apple Game Center. If we need to keep a copy of any email or social media interactions, we will obtain your permission beforehand.
